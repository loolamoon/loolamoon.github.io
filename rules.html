Library Rules

Also see Taking Care of Books and Checking Out Books.

  1. The library is a calm, quiet place to read and enjoy books.
  2. Respect the library and each other.
  3. Take care of library books, furniture, and equipment.
  4. Use a quiet voice and walk in the library.
  5. Use a shelf marker when you take a book from the shelf.
  6. Before you take a book out of the library, check it out at the front desk.

Taking Care of Books

  1. Handle library books gently.
  2. Keep library books clean.
  3. Keep library books away from babies and pets.
  4. Only use bookmarks in library books.
  5. If library books are damaged, bring them back to the library to be fixed. Don't try to fix them yourself or throw them away.

Checking Out Books

  • Kindergartners and 1st graders can check out 1 book at a time.
  • 2nd, 3rd, 4th, and 5th graders can check out 2 books at a time.
  • Students can keep books for 2 weeks and can renew their books once to keep them for 2 more weeks. After 2 weeks books become overdue.
  • Students don't have to pay anything for late books, but please return them as soon as you can!
  • Students with any overdue books may not check out any more books until they bring back the overdue book, or pay the book's price in cash to the main office.

The library is available for contact during school hours. Please don't hesitate to get in touch.
